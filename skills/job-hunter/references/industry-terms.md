# Industry Search Terms Reference

Use these tailored search terms when running job and people searches per industry.

## Fashion
- Keywords: "fashion marketing," "apparel brand," "RTW," "luxury fashion," "streetwear," "fashion week," "editorial," "wholesale marketing"
- Companies to target: NOBULL, J.Jill, New Balance, RUE GILT GROUPE, PVH, Ralph Lauren, LVMH, Tapestry
- Role variants: Brand Marketing Coordinator, Fashion Marketing Associate, Wholesale Marketing Coordinator, PR & Marketing Assistant

## Beauty / Makeup / Skincare
- Keywords: "beauty marketing," "cosmetics brand," "skincare campaigns," "prestige beauty," "mass beauty," "clean beauty," "influencer beauty"
- Companies to target: Estée Lauder, Coty, Ulta Beauty, L'Oréal, e.l.f. Beauty, Rare Beauty, Tatcha, Glossier, Fenty Beauty
- Role variants: Beauty Brand Coordinator, Beauty Marketing Associate, Digital Marketing Coordinator - Beauty, Retail Marketing Coordinator

## CPG (Consumer Packaged Goods)
- Keywords: "CPG marketing," "brand management," "shopper marketing," "trade marketing," "consumer insights"
- Companies to target: Unilever, P&G, Kraft Heinz, Nestlé, General Mills, Conagra
- Role variants: Assistant Brand Manager, Marketing Coordinator, Shopper Marketing Coordinator

## Tech / SaaS
- Keywords: "growth marketing," "demand gen," "lifecycle marketing," "product marketing," "B2B marketing," "email campaigns"
- Companies to target: HubSpot, Wayfair, Toast, Klaviyo, Drift, Brightcove, LogMeIn
- Role variants: Marketing Coordinator, Campaign Marketing Associate, Growth Marketing Analyst, Email Marketing Coordinator

## Lifestyle / Media
- Keywords: "lifestyle brand," "content marketing," "editorial marketing," "digital magazine," "influencer campaigns"
- Companies to target: Society19, Condé Nast, Hearst, BDG Media, Gallery Media Group
- Role variants: Digital Marketing Coordinator, Content & Marketing Associate, Social Media & Campaigns Coordinator

## Retail
- Keywords: "retail marketing," "store marketing," "omnichannel marketing," "visual merchandising," "in-store campaigns"
- Role variants: Marketing Coordinator, Retail Brand Coordinator, Omnichannel Marketing Assistant

## Sports / Athleisure
- Keywords: "sports marketing," "athlete partnerships," "performance brand," "activewear campaigns," "sports sponsorship"
- Companies to target: New Balance, NOBULL, PUMA, Tracksmith, Reebok, Under Armour
- Role variants: Sports Marketing Coordinator, Brand Marketing Associate, Partnerships Marketing Coordinator
